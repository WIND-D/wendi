DCE(deterministic construal errors) happens at a high frequency when people are looking at predictive interval forecast, where people misinterpreted the predictive interval as a description of diurnal fluctuation that is a deterministic forecast. Study found that when uncertainty explanation was combined with the forecast, successfully reduced DCEs.
