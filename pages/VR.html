AR is an anchor

Dynamic architecture model texturing

Project Type:  UX research & Design

Roles:  UX researcher & developer

Team:  Wendi Ding, Chaitrali Gandhi

Timeline:  Fall 2018

Design Challenge -
Disconnection in Real - Digital Architecture Texturing

Physical model | Digital Model

While choosing materials, Architects consider a lot of factors besides personal preference like visual display, durability, feeling, climate, environment, light, geographical orientation, the program (architecture functionality), cost, sustainability, security. However, it is difficult to understand all these factors with the help of a physical model. Communication between architect and client

Solution Overview

TexAR is a dynamic texturing method which would use AR to enable instant dynamic texturing on the physical model by recognizing the structure and let architect choose different materials from a material library. The physical model would be an anchor that connects textures and physical model. Architects can hold the physical model in their hands, see effects applying materials they choose in real time with our system and have a good spatial understanding at the same time. TexAR improves the communication accuracy and efficiency on architecture texturing, which enable the clients to get a deep understanding of the materials that will be applied on the model in the real world.
